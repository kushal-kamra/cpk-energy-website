Our Audit Methodology

  1. Pre-Audit Planning

    Collaborating with your team to define scope, objectives, and gather preliminary data.

  2. On-Site Diagnostics

    Conducting detailed fieldwork with advanced instrumentation to measure and map energy flows.

  3. SEC Benchmarking & Analysis

    Analyzing data to benchmark Specific Energy Consumption (SEC) against industry standards.

  4. Recommendations with ROI

    Presenting clear, actionable recommendations complete with technical details and ROI analysis.

  5. Final Report & Roadmap

    Delivering a comprehensive report and a strategic roadmap for phased implementation.
